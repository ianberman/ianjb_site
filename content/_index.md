      <input class="mobile-collapsible__toggle" type="checkbox" id="herndon-details-toggle" />

      <div class="mobile-collapsible__content">
        <p>Exhibitions working at the cutting edge of audio generation technology, developing a protocol for consensual training and reproduction of AI voice models.</p>
        <p>Installation spaces utilize bespoke multichannel d&b audiotechnik soundsystems for playback, requiring custom mixes of singing, AI generated music, and field recordings. The spaces are rigged to record public training ceremonies.
        </p>
        <p>Cleaned & mixed recordings are used to train experimental AI models in collaboration with IRCAM and the Algomus research group at Université de Lille. The documentation and results of this process are spatialized for each new exhibition. 
        </p>
